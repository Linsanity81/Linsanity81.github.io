I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Culture and Creativity, BNBU. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Computer Graphics Laboratory, Singapore University of Technology and Design, under the supervision of Prof. Peng Song, where I received my doctoral degree in June 2024. Prior to that, I earned my bachelor’s degree from Shantou University, supervised by Prof. Chuliang Wei. During 2019 to 2020, I was a research intern at CUHKSZ under the supervision of Prof. Zhenglong Sun.

Research Areas: Computer Graphics, Computational Design, VR/AR/MR
